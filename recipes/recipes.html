Recipes of Big Al's K.C. Bar-B-Q Sauce

Big Al's K.C. Bar-B-Q Sauce is a rich and bold sauce that is perfect for basting, dipping, or drizzling over your favorite meat. This sauce is a classic Kansas City-style barbecue sauce that is sweet and tangy with a hint of smokiness. It is easy to make and is sure to be a hit at your next barbecue.

  • 2 cups ketchup
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 ¼ cups brown sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups red wine vinegar
  • ½ cup unsulfured molasses
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 teaspoons hickory-flavored liquid smoke
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
